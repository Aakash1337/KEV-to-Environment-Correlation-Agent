About KEV Mapper

KEV Mapper is a local-first tool that correlates CISA's Known Exploited Vulnerabilities (KEV) catalog with your environment. It provides:

  • Automated KEV ingestion and tracking
  • Deterministic matching with scanner findings
  • AI-assisted remediation guidance
  • Priority-based work queues
  • Export capabilities (Markdown, JSON, CSV)
